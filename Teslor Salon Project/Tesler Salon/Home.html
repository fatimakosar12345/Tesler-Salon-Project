Our Founder

      Emmy-nominated celebrity hair and make-up artist Adriana Tesler has garnered a reputation for being the industry’s best kept secret for many years. Voted “Best Blow-out in LA” in Allure Magazine’s Best of the Best two years in a row, her talent has taken her to some of the most illustrious facets of the industry. Whether at New York Fashion Week alongside Guido Paulo, Didier Malige, or Anthony Turner, to gracing the covers of international publications, such as Vogue (British, Italia, Turkey), with renowned photographers Peter Lindbergh, Terry Richardson, Willy Vanderperre, and Alasdair McLellan, or television shows, and red carpets for Kate Beckinsale, Molly Sims, Paris Hilton, Nicky Hilton, Rita Wilson, Jen Meyer, Zoe Saldana, Sofia Vergara, or Tamera Mowry, Adriana is considered one of the best talents in the industry. Her salon experience covers some of the most acclaimed salons, from the prestigious Art Luna in Santa Monica, to the famed Neil George, and working under celebrity colorist Tracey Cunningham in Beverly Hills. Adriana created Tesler Salon with the intent to bring the highest level of services she provides to the Silver Lake area, and has assembled a superstar team of artists in that endeavor. She is known for her beautiful highlights and balayage, outstanding and lasting blow-outs, and pristine make-up.
